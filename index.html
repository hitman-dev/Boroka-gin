01

The Classic Blue

Timeless simplicity

  • 1 Boróka Barely Blue (275ml)
  • 3 Ice cubes
  • 1 Orange wheel
  • 1 Sprig of rosemary

Pour Boróka over ice in a copa glass. Garnish with orange and rosemary. Let the blue mesmerise.

Easy5 min
02

Blue Sunset Spritz

Golden hour in a glass

  • 1 Boróka Barely Blue
  • 30ml Fresh grapefruit juice
  • 15ml Elderflower syrup
  • Top Sparkling water

Combine Boróka, grapefruit juice, and elderflower syrup over ice. Top with sparkling water.

Medium8 min
03

Midnight Tide

Deep. Dark. Delicious.

  • 1 Boróka Barely Blue
  • 20ml Coconut cream
  • 15ml Lime juice
  • Dash Butterfly pea flower tea

Shake coconut cream and lime with ice. Add Boróka, float butterfly pea tea for a stunning layer effect.

Advanced10 min
04

Blue Lagoon Fizz

Party starter perfection

  • 1 Boróka Barely Blue
  • 30ml Pineapple juice
  • 15ml Vanilla syrup
  • 1 Mint sprig + edible glitter

Muddle mint with vanilla syrup. Add Boróka and pineapple juice over ice. Top with edible glitter.

Easy7 min
